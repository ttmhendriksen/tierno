These things that you might expect to be there wont be:
- Horses - they do exist, but have not been bred to support heavy armored figures, nor have stirrups been invented. If you're small and lightly armored there may be options here. 
- Steel - ironworking is at its infancy (requiring better furnaces), resulting in low quality material. 
- Paper - not yet invented. Papyrus, parchment & clay tablets are used instead
