Short rest take 1 night and require you to use at least 1 hit die if you want to recharge your abilities. 
Long rest require 3 consecutive nights in a safe and comfortable place (for example, beds in a known city)

Spells and other features with a longer duration will be adjusted on a case-by-case basis to fit this system. I.e. if they are assumed to last for the full dy, theymake have to be adjusted to a week etc.

The main goals of this include:
- spread out encounters and such throughout a week instead of a day, allowing for a more natural pace while travelling
- Make cities and safe spaces a valuable commodity, making the unknown more dangerous and making discovering new places extra rewarding
- Make it so that you can't just use all abilities in one encounter, instead encouraging tactical play and making the times you do use your strongest abilities much more important and impactful
